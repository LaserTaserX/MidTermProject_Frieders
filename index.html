The Fantastical Self-Help Story of Brady Chan

Composed by Alexander Frieders

Hello! This fine gentleman is a dear friend of mine. However, he has been struggling with his mental competence for quite some time. As his friend, I have tasked you with a journey to help him regain his mental prowess. You will be presented with choices that will affect the outcome of the story. Choose correctly, as he is so stupid that one wrong choice could destroy everything he has left of his mental capacity. (If there is any left at all.)
